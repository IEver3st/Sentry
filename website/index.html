FAQ

Quick answers

The essentials, at a glance.

How big is Sentry?

The Windows installer is about 23 MB. No browser bundle, no extra bloat.

How much memory does it use?

Idle footprint stays under 10 MB of RAM while watching for schedules and triggers.

Can I restore partially?

Yes. Choose specific files or sets; manifests keep versions tidy for selective restores.

Does it auto-start and stay quiet?

You can enable start-on-boot and keep Sentry minimized; it runs in the background without popups.

How do updates work?

Signed releases from GitHub; you can auto-apply updates or trigger them manually from Settings.

Will it pause during heavy use?

You can throttle or schedule backups for off-hours; runs are lightweight and avoid spiking CPU.
